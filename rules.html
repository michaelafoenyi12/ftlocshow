FTLOC Official Game Rules

Welcome to the official FTLOC Rules page.

1. Overview

FTLOC is an entertainment platform that includes video episodes and a skill-based game show. Participants register through the official website and may be selected to participate in live game sessions.

2. Registration

Registration is free unless otherwise stated. Participants must provide accurate information. Duplicate or fraudulent entries may be disqualified.

3. Participant Selection

Participants are selected randomly from the list of registered users. Selection does not guarantee participation unless officially contacted.

4. Live Participation Requirements

Selected participants must: - Join using the provided link. - Have their camera turned on. - Follow all instructions during the session. Failure to comply may result in disqualification.

5. Episode Question Round

Each participant will select a number corresponding to available episode questions. The participant must answer correctly to qualify for the box selection round. Only one attempt per question is allowed unless otherwise stated.

6. FTLOC Box Round

The game includes five boxes labeled: F, T, L, O, C.

One box contains the prize. The winning box is randomly selected and documented before the game round. The selection is revealed after the contestant completes their turn.

7. Prize Distribution

Confirmed winners will receive their prize within 48 hours after official verification of the result. Prize delivery method will be communicated directly to the winner.

8. Fairness & Documentation

All winning box selections are documented prior to gameplay. Records are maintained to ensure transparency and fairness.

9. Eligibility

Participants must comply with all local laws. Additional eligibility requirements may be added in the future.

10. Rule Changes

FTLOC reserves the right to update rules at any time. Changes will be posted on the official website.
